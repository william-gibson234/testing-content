> **De Morgan’s Laws**
> - $(A\cup B)^c = A^c \cap B^c$
> 
> - $(A\cap B)^c = A^c \cup B^c$
> In other words: you can distribute “ $^c$ ” but must simultaneously do a switch $\cap\leftrightarrow \cup$.
