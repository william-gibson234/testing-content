> [!extra] Rules for sets
> **Algebraic rules**
> - Associativity: $(A\cup B)\cup C=A\cup (B\cup C)$. Analogous to $(A+B)+C=A+(B+C)$.
> 
> - Distributivity: $A\cap (B\cup C)=(A\cap B) \; \cup \; (A\cap C)$. Analogous to $A(B+C)=AB+AC$.
