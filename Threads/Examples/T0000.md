*Complex events:* for example: $A\cap B=\{HT\}$, or in words: 

$$
\text{“first was heads”}\;\text{AND}\;\text{“exactly one heads”}=\text{“heads-then-tails”}
$$

 Notice that the last one is a *complete description*, namely the *outcome* $HT$.
$A\cup B=\{HH,\,HT,\,TH\}$, or in words: 

$$
\begin{gather*}\text{“first was heads”}\;\text{OR}\;\text{“exactly one heads”}\\=\text{“starts with heads, else it’s tails-then-heads”}\end{gather*}
$$
